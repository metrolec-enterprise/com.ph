From Intercoms to DC Power Supplies

Metrolec Enterprise was established in Quezon City Philippines in 1993. The company started out as a small trading company specializing in trade of electrical substations, power centers and specialized electronic products. As the years passed-by, the company slowly shifted to manufacturing of intercom system and power supplies for elevator industry.
